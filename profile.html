    Universal Tube and Plastic Industries Ltd

    Universal Tube and Plastic Industries Ltd, an ISO 9001:2008 accredited company, is a leading manufacturer and distributor of High Frequency Induction Welded Steel Pipes and Tubes Established in 1990 and with manufacturing facilities in Jabel Ali and Dubai

    Investment Park, UTP produces over 180,000 metric tons annually. Spread over 25,000 square meters, and having a dedicated workforce of about 300 employees the company is equipped with the state of art machinery to manufacture world class welded steel pipes and tubes. UTP product line ranges in size from ½” to 8” in Rounds, 19 x 19 to 100 x 100 in Squares and 40 x 20 to 150 x 75 in Rectangular tubes.

    UTP is certified by:

    - BM Trade For ISO 9001:2008.

    - BSI UK for “PED ” (Pressure Equipment Directive)

    - TUV Nord for CE Mark for BS EN10255 & EN10219 for both Black & Galvanized steel pipes.

    - Sai Global for manufacture of Black & Galvanized steel pipes as per Australian Standards AS 1074.

    - Listed by UL for manufacture of ASTM A53 sch.40 Grade A, Grade B & as per ASTM A 795 Grade A & ASTM A 135 sch. 10 Grade A & B for Black & Galvanized steel pipes for Fire protection systems, Sprinkler systems and Chilled water systems.

    - Approved by ministry of Civil Defense U.A.E & Qatar for supply of pipes in U.A.E. & Qatar for MEP projects.
